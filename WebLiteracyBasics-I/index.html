Welcome to Club Testing!

Thanks for joining in the experiment. Here are the activities we're testing for local clubs and info on the process.

  • The Testing Process

    We recommend 45min. to teach each activity with prep time to try it yourself first. Feel free to test one or both activities, whatever works for you. If you also have the capacity to film or take photos of your tests, those would be amazing resources.

    There are also brief pre & post questionnaires to complete for each activity. Once you've tested the activities, we'll wrap up with a written reflection.

    Each section will have a check-in call to share notes among the club testers. Email teachtheweb@mozillafoundation.org at any time. As always, we welcome your feedback to make this process better and work for you!

    Web Literacy Basics

    The first module we're testing is Web Literacy Basics I with three sections: 1) Reading the Web, 2) Writing the Web, and 3) Participating on the Web. Learners will discover the foundations of the web through production and collaboration. The learning objectives underpinning each session are taken from the competencies of Mozilla's community-created Web Literacy Map.
